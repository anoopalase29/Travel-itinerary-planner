7-Day Overview — Japan

Day 1 — Arrival: Tokyo

  • Arrive, check in, rest. Evening walk in Shinjuku or Shibuya crossing.
  • Light dinner: ramen or izakaya.

Day 2 — Tokyo Highlights

  • Morning: Senso-ji in Asakusa, Nakamise shopping street.
  • Afternoon: Ueno Park or Akihabara.
  • Evening: Shibuya/Harajuku for shops and street food.

Day 3 — Day Trip (Hakone or Nikko)

  • Choose Hakone for hot springs and views of Mt. Fuji, or Nikko for shrines and nature.

Day 4 — Travel to Kyoto

  • Shinkansen to Kyoto. Afternoon: Fushimi Inari Taisha.

Day 5 — Kyoto Temples & Gardens

  • Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion), Ryoan-ji, Arashiyama bamboo grove.

Day 6 — Nara or Osaka Day Trip

  • Nara: Todai-ji, deer park. Or Osaka: Dotonbori & street food.

Day 7 — Final Day & Departure

  • Last-minute shopping, sweets, head to airport. Tip: check train times.

Quick Tips

Transport

Get a JR Pass if doing long-distance travel; use Suica/Pasmo for cities.

Money & Etiquette

Carry some cash. Be quiet on public transport and remove shoes when required.

Packing

Comfortable shoes, adapter, light layers by season.
