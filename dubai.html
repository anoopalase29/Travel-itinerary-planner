5-Day Overview — Dubai

Day 1 — Arrival & Marina

  • Check in, relax. Evening: Dubai Marina walk and dinner by the water.

Day 2 — Old Dubai & Souks

  • Al Fahidi, Dubai Museum, abra crossing and Gold/Spice souks.

Day 3 — Modern Dubai

  • Dubai Mall, Aquarium & Burj Khalifa. Evening fountain show.

Day 4 — Desert Safari

  • Dune bashing, camel ride, BBQ dinner and cultural show.

Day 5 — Relax & Depart

  • Beach or hotel pool, last-minute shopping, depart.

Quick Tips

Climate

Hot year-round. Pack sun protection and light clothes; respect local dress codes.

Transport

Taxis and Metro are convenient; distances can be large.

Culture

Respect customs, avoid public displays of affection, and observe Ramadan rules when applicable.
