6-Day Overview — Paris

Day 1 — Arrival & Evening Stroll

  • Check-in, relax. Evening walk near the Seine and Notre-Dame area.

Day 2 — Classic Paris

  • Louvre, Tuileries, Champs-Élysées to Arc de Triomphe.

Day 3 — Eiffel Tower & River Cruise

  • Eiffel Tower visit and Seine cruise in the evening.

Day 4 — Montmartre & Museums

  • Sacré-Cœur and artists' square; Musée d'Orsay option.

Day 5 — Versailles

  • Full day at Palace of Versailles and gardens.

Day 6 — Food & Depart

  • Markets, pastries, last-minute shopping, depart.

Quick Tips

Tickets

Reserve museum and Eiffel tickets online to avoid queues.

Getting Around

Metro is best — buy a carnet for multiple rides.

Etiquette

Learn basic French greetings — locals appreciate it.
